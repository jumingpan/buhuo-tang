About

不惑堂 · The Hall of No Doubts

Bu Huo Tang — "the hall of no doubts" — is a private dealership specializing in fine Chinese art, classical antiques, and objects of scholarly refinement. Each piece is personally sourced, authenticated, and presented with full provenance.

We serve collectors, institutions, and connoisseurs seeking works of enduring quality and cultural significance. Our collection spans ceramics, ink paintings, classical furniture, and the full range of the literati tradition.

Based in New Jersey, we serve clients across the United States and internationally. Inquiries are welcomed for individual pieces or for guidance on building a collection.
